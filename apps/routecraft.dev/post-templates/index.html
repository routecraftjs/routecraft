Post templates gallery

Every in-feed template, previewed at the width LinkedIn renders on a phone, so you can judge legibility before posting. Toggle the theme to preview the light or dark export.

Previews are live iframes of the actual template files. To export a post: open a template with its Open ↗ link, then capture the .frame element. In Chrome devtools, right-click the .frame node and choose Capture node screenshot for a pixel-exact PNG at full size. Append ?theme=dark to the URL for the dark variant.
